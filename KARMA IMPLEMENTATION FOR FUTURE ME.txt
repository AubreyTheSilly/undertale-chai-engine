km values
bone = 6
blaster = 10
bone stabs = 6
boneloop = 6
button bones = 1
choice bones = 2

attacks increase km by their km value with no invincibility frames triggered. DO NOT DEPLETE HP! ONLY INCREASE KARMA

notes
karma must always be one less than the player's HP and cannot be more than 40!!

if inv is 45 or more, km_bonus has a 1 in 2 chance to be 1
if inv is 60 or more, km_bonus has a 2 in 3 chance to be 1
if inv is 75 or more, km_bonus is force set to 1

km_requirement is 1 by default
if km is 40 or more, km_requirement should be 1
if km is 30 or more, km_requirement should be 2 and multiply km_bonus by 2
if km is 20 or more, km_requirement should be 5 and multiply km_bonus by 5
if km is 10 or more, km_requirement should be 15 and multiply km_bonus by 5
OTHERWISE SET KM_REQUIREMENT TO 30 AND MULTIPLY KM_BONUS BY 10!!!

km_t increments every frame
if km_t is more than km_requirement+km_bonus, and hp is same as prevhp (not sure why that check is here but it's in the code so i'm noting it!), then reset km_t and remove 1 hp and 1 km

if HP will be 0 after km depletes, set hp to 1 and reset km!

at end of frame set prevhp to current hp
